Pricing your website with Atlas Reach

When it comes to pricing we strive to have transparent and easy to understand pricing schemas so you always know what to expect for your wallet. For your convinience our pricing model is broken down into three easy to understand brackets.


Simple Website

$300

We categorize a simple website as a site that includes five pages or less. This kind of site can include an unlimited amount of content so long as everything fits on five pages.

Extra Pages

If you find that you need more than five pages but less than fifteen pages we offer an extra five pages for an additional fee. For an additional $150 you can add up to five additional pages for a total of ten pages for your simple website.


Complex Website

$600

We categorize a complex website as a site that includes fifteen pages or more. This kind of site can include an unlimited amount of content so long as everything fits on fifteen pages.

Extra Pages

If you find that you need more than fifteen pages we offer an additional five pages for a small fee. Each additional five pages cost an additional $150


E-commerce Website

$1500

We categorize an e-commerce website as a site that includes an online store front. This kind of site can include an unlimited amount of content.
